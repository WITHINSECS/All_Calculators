# Calculator Hub

## Overview

Welcome to our **Calculator Hub**! We offer a range of interactive calculators designed to help you with financial, health, lifestyle, and other essential calculations. Each calculator has been built to be user-friendly and provides accurate results. Browse through the different calculators below to get started!

---

## Available Calculators

### 1. **Mortgage House Affordability Calculator**

#### Overview:
This calculator helps you estimate the maximum home price you can afford based on your income, monthly debts, down payment, interest rate, loan term, and other factors. It calculates the monthly mortgage payment, including taxes, insurance, and PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) if applicable.

#### Features:
- **Maximum Home Price Calculation**: Estimate the home price you can afford.
- **Monthly Payment Breakdown**: See how your payments are distributed (Principal & Interest, Taxes, PMI, Insurance, HOA Dues).
- **Pie Chart**: Visual representation of payment distribution.
- **Customizable Input**: Adjust inputs like annual income, interest rate, loan term, and more.

---

### 2. **Car Loan Calculator**

#### Overview:
This calculator estimates your monthly payments for a car loan based on your loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. It helps you understand how much you will pay each month and the total amount paid over the loan term.

#### Features:
- **Loan Term Selection**: Choose the number of years for your loan.
- **Interest Rate Input**: Specify the interest rate for the loan.
- **Payment Breakdown**: See the breakdown of the monthly payment (principal vs interest).
- **Loan Amortization Chart**: Visual representation of how your loan balance decreases over time.

---

### 3. **Retirement Savings Calculator**

#### Overview:
This tool helps you estimate how much you need to save each month for retirement based on your current savings, expected rate of return, retirement age, and more. It also lets you include employer contributions like 401(k) matches.

#### Features:
- **Monthly Contribution Calculation**: Find out how much you need to save monthly.
- **Total Savings Estimate**: See how much you will have saved by your retirement age.
- **Employer Match**: Include employer contributions like 401(k) matches.
- **Visual Progress**: Track how your savings grow over time with line and pie charts.

---

### 4. **BMI Calculator**

#### Overview:
This simple calculator helps you calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) based on your weight and height. It helps you assess whether you're in a healthy weight range.

#### Features:
- **Simple Input**: Enter your weight and height to calculate your BMI.
- **BMI Categories**: It classifies your BMI into categories like underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.
- **Health Insights**: Get tips on maintaining a healthy weight based on your BMI.

---

### 5. **Loan EMI Calculator**

#### Overview:
Estimate your monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) for a loan based on the loan amount, tenure, and interest rate. This is helpful for personal loans, home loans, or car loans.

#### Features:
- **Loan Amount**: Enter the loan amount you wish to borrow.
- **Interest Rate**: Specify the annual interest rate.
- **Tenure**: Choose the number of months or years for the loan repayment.
- **EMI Breakdown**: Get a breakdown of principal vs interest for each EMI.

---

## Technologies Used

All our calculators are built using the following technologies:

- **React**: For building the interactive user interface.
- **TypeScript**: For ensuring type safety and a better development experience.
- **Recharts**: For creating visually appealing charts like pie and line charts.
- **TailwindCSS**: For responsive and modern UI design.
- **React Hooks**: For managing component states and handling user inputs efficiently.
